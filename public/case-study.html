Candidate case study

Ujala Agarwal built LumenStack AI to turn repositories into clear engineering narratives.

This case study is written for recruiters and hiring teams who want to evaluate product sense, full-stack execution, AI integration, and deployment readiness quickly.

Problem

Repositories are hard to review quickly. Recruiters and teams need signal without reading every file.

Solution

Analyze code structure, summarize architecture, generate diagrams, and expose everything through a polished product UI.

Impact

The app communicates technical depth, design taste, backend capability, and AI workflow thinking in one demo.

Engineering decisions

Express services are separated around source preparation, analysis, AI, comparison, chat, and session storage. Fallback AI behavior keeps demos stable even without API quota.

Recruiter decisions

The UI exposes proof fast: candidate links, saved projects, architecture cockpit, live analyzer, README, deployment config, and clear API routes.
